ABOUT US


Our Company

We want to be the first place you picture in your mind when you think of pasta. D'Arte - a modern eatery with fresh food and a place where you can rediscover Italian classics served with perfection.

With D'Arte, we aim to change the face of fine dining and provide our guests with an uplifting experience, and of course, delicious and fresh Italian food. Everything at D'Arte, from the pasta to the lasagna, is prepared with the finest ingredients coupled with unmatched passion for the art of cooking.

 

Our Story

Owner Steve Chabot first opened the doors to D'Arte in 2014. The name of the restaurant was inspired by his fondness and creativity of food.

He was always passionate about making Italian classics and wanted to make the best quality Italian food. He viewed Singapore as having little to none italian authenticity and wanted to bring affordable and delicate itlian food here for the community.
